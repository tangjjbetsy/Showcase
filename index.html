Audio Samples: Midi Synthesis Through VALLE Model

We have selected four different segments from various performances and compositions, organized into five distinct groups of samples. For each group, five types of audio are presented:

  1. Groundtruth: The original audio segment from the performance.
  2. Encodec: Audio reconstructed by the 32khz Encodec model, which was pretrained by facebook.
  3. Encodec-FT: Audio reconstructed by the 32khz Encodec model, which was fine-tuned for 20 epoches by 65 hours of piano performances from the ATEPP dataset.
  4. Vall-E + Encodec: Audio generated by the VALLE model, where MIDI files were tokenized using a method similar to the one used in the score-to-audio project. The model was prompted with the first 3 seconds of the audio segment and midi tokens.
  5. Vall-E + Encodec-FT: Audio generated by the VALLE model, using the fine-tuned Encodec as audio tokenizer.
